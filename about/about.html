About Us

Nairobi Technical Training Institute (NTTI) is a leading technical training institution located in Nairobi, Kenya. Established in 1980, NTTI has been providing quality technical education and training to students from diverse backgrounds.

Our institute offers a wide range of technical courses designed to equip students with practical skills and knowledge that are relevant to today's industries. With state-of-the-art facilities and experienced faculty members, we strive to ensure that our students are well-prepared for successful careers in their chosen fields.

At NTTI, we are committed to promoting innovation, excellence, and professionalism in technical education. We believe in fostering a supportive learning environment where students can thrive and reach their full potential.

Whether you are looking to start a new career or enhance your existing skills, NTTI welcomes you to join our community and embark on a rewarding educational journey.
